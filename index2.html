I'm Ravi, with over 9 years in designing and develop successful human-centered experiences, understanding stories, emotions and expectations.

What I DO?

I like turning complex problems into simple, attractive, intuitive interface design & develop compelling apps. I think it is significant search for the right problems to resolve, always trying to merge the potential user needs with the company aims. I take UX design as a endless accomplishment of all those small details that create one, big device.Every touch point matters.With business knowledge, IT background and human-centered approach I help both startups and large companies to fill in the gap between users and technology. Help companies better understand the desires and values of their customers as they relate to an organizations planned goals, product/service development efforts, customer engagement inspirations and brand reputation.

What I Approach?

After thorough analysis of business goals and user needs, I design solutions that ensure that the right content, features and functionality are presented in the right place, at the right time, in the right way, thereby providing experiences that engage people and produce meaningful results, effortless interaction, and ultimately business benefits. My approach is to create better interfaces and experiences for the user, making the web, applications and technology easier for the user. I like the challenge of figuring out the best way to solve the users’ problems. I use research, workshops, documentation, observation, and yes, a lot of post-it notes and other methods to initiate design thinking and the problem-solving process (cookies also help).

How I do UX Process?

  • Research- You cannot create a great experience if you don’t understand what you are designing and who you are designing it for. Learn about your audience. Learn about your stakeholders. Learn about the competition. Learn everything you possibly can about the current landscape surrounding the product. Talk to people. Identify their needs, pain points, what they like, despise and what they think could improve the overall situation. Audit, Competitive analysis, Benchmark,User Target, Analysis and Survey / interviews
  • Strategy- Using that information, I will strategically organize the site/ app content throughout the pages of the site/ app. I will also plan how your users can move though and interact with your site/app.I believe that great design does more than just add visual beauty. I believe it can help solve problems and accomplish aims.
  • Ideate- Ok now we have a pretty good idea of what the issues are, it’s time to start making hypotheses. Generating ideas about how we might fix the problem. Nothing is to crazy here. The wild ideas are the ones that usually lead to something awesome and original.Personas, User journey and Fonctionnality. Create brainstorming sessions and design studios. Lots of postits and whiteboard sketches that have a life span of about 4 minutes until you scrap it for a better idea. Once the ideas start flowing, the solutions will start to show themselves.
  • Refine- Take your hypotheses and refine them. At this stage you will notice which ones are clearly not going to work and others that have great potential. Some will be in between, park those ideas and come back to them later if needed. You only need to refine them to a stage where you can validate them.
  • Wireframes-Then, I will create a layout and interaction design that will allow users to move through your site/ app in the most effective way.
  • UI Design- Once the final layout has been decided upon I will add in colours and typography that best represent your brand.I help users accomplish their goals while interacting with your business.
  • Front-end - Once the final layout has been decided upon I will add in colours and typography that best represent your brand.I help users accomplish their goals while interacting with your business.
  • Testing- If your time and/or budget allows I will help you devise a testing strategy. This will allow us to check how your users understand the content and the layout design of the site/app. Based on the results I will update the design.Testing can come before or after the UI design depending on the needs of the project.
