Landing Pages with More Consistency and Structure

Neatly modern landing pages look. All the content (blocks with texts, photos, etc.) is placed on a single page, so that a visitor doesn’t have to move from one page to another.Visitors will no longer need to navigate between multiple pages, but can instead just scroll from top to bottom.Seamless interface is the hottest UI trend.

Unique images

With any search engine, to minimise the chance of being penalised for having the same images and copy as others, 2018 is the year to find unique and abstract images to incorporate onto your website. The images you select for your overhaul need to be of high quality to ensure you’re able to display a product that’s of very high quality.

Unique lettering style

Typography is another clearly defined trend. Use massive headings, don’t be shy to play with font types. Don’t forget about animation, but try to make is as smooth and soft-core as possible. Care of the structure of text blocks and their positioning on the page. Great typographic treatment eliminates the need in other types of content.Typography is feature to experiment when designing personalized app designs. When added with images, colors, video, animations, and innovative layouts, the element brings about exceptional user experience

Gradient colors

Modern displays are capable of reproducing much more color shades than previous-gen screens, so why not take advantage of it? Gradients (smooth subtle color transitions) are all the rage now, so feel free to use them to highlight the beauty of your website design.The screens now are able to generate more and more colour variations so utilizing that is a wise decision. So, trying a new color variation may create a trendy look for your website.

Video content

Videos in 2018 are going to be even more popular than graphics, and not without reason. Few people like reading huge blocks of text, while video clips are perfect for rendering a lot of information quickly. Moreover, they are easy to share in social media. If possible, make unique video clips specially for your business promotion. But try not to use mainstream videos they usually are of no interest.Video contents are said to be more engaging than any other content. So not for just promoting brand and products, videos are becoming a trend.

Custom illustration

Website is Mobile Responsive

Ensuring your website is mobile responsive is something that’ll never get old. As most people are constantly on the go, to ensure you are able to reach a larger target audience, having a website that isn’t mobile responsive will disadvantage you and potentially increase your bounce rate. Businesses should constantly be working on their ability to have a website that is mobile responsive. By introducing higher quality images and graphics to your website, tests should be conducted to check that the load time of the website isn’t too long and the website becomes responsive.

Vibrant Colors & Borderless Display

Valiant and try out newer colors, but select and apply them wisely. Make sure the colors allow to create unique, powerful, and eye-friendly experience. Borderless Display introduced the trend of transforming the usage of strict flat design ideas in mobile apps, boosting fluent storytelling.

Final thoughts

User Interface is a crucial element of a web or mobile app. Briefly speaking, interface is the face of your product; it is what makes visitors stay on your site or leave it after a while. Every visitor is your potential customer, and to a large extent his or her first impression determines whether he or she will turn into a loyal customer
